Why We Do It

Somebody opened a door for us. Now we're holding it open for others.

Accoladi is personal.

It began with a simple belief: every performing arts student deserves a plan, a mentor, and someone who believes their future is possible.

Founder Dr. Randall Bayne was a first-generation college student whose music teachers showed him how talent could become college opportunity.

As a public school educator, he passed that guidance on to his own students and watched opportunity grow when someone took the time to believe in them.

Later, as a college dean, he saw how many talented students were still waiting to be seen.

Accoladi was created to change that.

Today, Accoladi carries that promise forward by helping students, educators, recruiters, and directors connect talent with the opportunities it deserves.
