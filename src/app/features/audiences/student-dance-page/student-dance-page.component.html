School District Subscription

Accoladi.com provides school districts with the opportunity to purchase discounted subscriptions for all their middle and high school performing arts students. The following districts have already secured annual subscriptions for their students:

  • Spring Independent School District (Houston, Texas)
  • Klein Independent School District (Klein, Texas)
  • Tomball Independent School District (Tomball, Texas)
  • Sheldon Independent School District (Houston, Texas)
  • Fort Bend Independent School District (Sugar Land, Texas)

If you are a middle or high school performing arts student in one of these districts, your school has already purchased a subscription for you. Contact your dance teacher for details.
