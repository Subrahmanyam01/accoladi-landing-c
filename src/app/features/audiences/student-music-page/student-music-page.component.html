Middle & High School Musician

Great musicians are everywhere. Being heard is the hard part.

WHY

You have spent years building your musicianship. The question is not whether you have talent. The question is whether the right people have seen it yet.

HOW

Accoladi helps you showcase your training, repertoire, achievements, and performance videos so colleges, scholarship providers, military ensembles, and recruiters can discover you sooner.

WHAT

A free performer profile and portfolio with optional tools that help turn musical ability into visibility, connection, and opportunity.

Your talent is real. Accoladi helps the right ears find it.

School District Subscription

Accoladi.com provides school districts with the opportunity to purchase discounted subscriptions for all their middle and high school performing arts students. The following districts have already secured annual subscriptions for their students:

  • Spring Independent School District (Houston, Texas)
  • Klein Independent School District (Klein, Texas)
  • Tomball Independent School District (Tomball, Texas)
  • Sheldon Independent School District (Houston, Texas)
  • Fort Bend Independent School District (Sugar Land, Texas)

If you are a middle or high school performing arts student in one of these districts, your school has already purchased a subscription for you. Contact your music teacher for details.
